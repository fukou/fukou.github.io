About the project

I've been making a Tumblr theme since 2011. It was a long journey for me when it comes to coding. I joined Tumblr in 2011 for blogging and discovered a theme made by atmospherica (deactived)


I really like her design so I used it on my blog and was playing around with the theme, familiarizing the structure of the theme. I am entirely self-taught....After doing some trial-and-error, I was confident enough to create my own themes for personal use and decided to make a blog theme to publish my creations.


Fukuo comes from the name of Fukuoka—it is Japan's 6th largest city; I decided to choose this username because it sounds familiar. This blog has been my source of learning and I am so grateful that this blog is popular enough in the community. I have made some tutorials that is web design-related and the theme I made have been used for more than +142.000 users since the beginning of that blog.
